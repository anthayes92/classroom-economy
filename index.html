About Classroom Economy

📚

Educational

Students learn financial literacy through hands-on experience with earning, spending, and budgeting.

🎯

Engaging

Gamified learning experience that motivates students to participate and excel in classroom activities.

📊

Trackable

Teachers can monitor student progress and adjust the economy to meet learning objectives.

Key Features

💳 Student Accounts

Each student gets their own account to track earnings and spending

📈 Transaction History

Complete record of all transactions for transparency and learning

👨‍🏫 Admin Dashboard

Teachers can manage all accounts and approve transactions

🎁 Rewards System

Earn currency for good behavior, completed assignments, and participation
